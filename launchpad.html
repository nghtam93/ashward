About

ASC is an essential part of The Ashward platform. It is an BEP-20 utility token built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) that serves as the basis for transactions within Ashward, and has the two main uses: Access Ashward platform and staking. ASC has a fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 (one billion) tokens.
